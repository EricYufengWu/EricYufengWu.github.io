Pop-up Birthday Cake

October 2018

This is a gift I created for my best friend's 19-year-old birthday - a pop-up birthday cake! Inspired by some amazing pop-u[p artworks made by artists around the world, I decided to make my own pop-up book.

After sketching out ideas on my ipad, I created the CAD file in Adobe Illustrator, which I then fabricated using a laser-cutter and assembled together using paper glue. It was challenging to manipulate delicate paper and fold them into desired shapes, but it was also a fun and rewarding experience.
