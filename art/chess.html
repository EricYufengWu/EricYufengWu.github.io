Chinese Chess

October 2018

This is a Chinese chess set that is entirely made up of laser-cutted plywood. The chess grid also acts like a little stool or table and is covered by clear wood varnish. 4 pieces of laser-cut plywood were pressure-fitted on to the larger piece of wood as stands. All the Chinese calligraphy characters on the chess were handwritten by me and were traced in Adobe Illustrator to get engraved on plywood.
