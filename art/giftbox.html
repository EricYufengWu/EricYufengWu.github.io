Gift Box

October 2019

As part of my annual gift-making heist for my best friend's birthday, this year I made her a custom gift box to store two parts of the gift together:

"Something that might actually be useful" - a laptop stand.

"Something useless, but perhapse a bit more interesting" - 20 laser-cutted keychains with 20 of her favorite things engraved on top.

The entire project was designed in Adobe Illustrator from scratch and fabricated with a lasercutter under 10 hours.
