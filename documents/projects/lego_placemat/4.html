One-Page Building Instruction

As part of my summer research project in 2018, I experimented with possible ways to shrink a LEGO instruction from a booklet to a single-sided paper. I used Stud.io to build the LEGO models digitally, and I generated exploded views, and used Indesign to produce a final PDF for the instruction.
