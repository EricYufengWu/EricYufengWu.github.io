LEGO SEC at Tufts
Fall 2018

This Advanced LEGO model features is a model of the Science and Engineering Complex (SEC) at Tufts University. I first designed this model in Stud.io , a LEGO CAD software (I had limited access to the building dimensions; basically Google Maps and pictures I took were what I had). After that, all the pieces were bought and assembled together.

The model was presented to the dean of the School of Engineering at Tufts as a gift from Tufts Center for Engineering Education and Outreach (CEEO). It went viral through the school's Facebook and Twitter post, and It also got featured as fornt cover on the Tufts Mechanical Engineering Department Annual Report.
