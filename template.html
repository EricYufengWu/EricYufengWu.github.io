Hexapods

Starting in my Sophomore Spring, I am building a series of hexapods from scratch. Right now I have built and tested 4 of them, and the number will continue to grow as I keep making improvements on all aspects.

LEGO Spike Prime Backpack

As an intern in Tufts Center for Engineering Education and Outreach (CEEO), I designed PCBs and 3D-printed housings that combine 3rd-party devices with the new LEGO robotics education kit.

Delta Ceramics 3D-Printer

With a professor in Tufts Sculpture department, I built a delta-style 3D-printer dedicated for ceramics (clay) printing. We adopted a design online and built our own version with improvements.

Fluidic Control Board for Soft Robotics

I built a control board with a solenoid valve manifold and power MOSFETS. The board is used in my lab to study and test out inflatable soft robotics actuators.
