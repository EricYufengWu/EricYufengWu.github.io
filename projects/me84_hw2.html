HW 2 - Haptic Feedback

Spring 2019

This is a LEGO toy car with wireless joystick control. Bluetooth communication is set between two EV3 bricks. The haptic feedback of vibration is achieved by rotating an uneven weight at the center of the joystick. This robot was programmed in LabVIEW.
