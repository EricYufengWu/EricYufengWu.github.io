H1 Mini

August 2019

This robot is more like a "branch off" from my "H" series hexapods - after finishing H1, I decided to build a similar robot but make it as small and compact as I possibly can. And here's the outcome: maximum width of 12cm (~4in) and a weight less than 120g (~4oz).

The robot consists of 6 3.7g micro analog servos and 6 1.2g linear servos. It runs with an Adafruit Feather Hazzah and has the Adafruit PCA9685 servo shield stacked under the main body. The legs were 3D printed with a Prusia MK3S with 100% infill.
